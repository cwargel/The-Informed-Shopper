About Us

The Informed Shopper was created to help consumers make the best use of their money. With so many competing companies, and so many different devices on the market today, the decisions available can be overwhelming. Using reviews from fellow consumers, and information from experts, shoppers can determine what they'd like the most and make a purchase they won't regret.

We collect most of our information from customer reviews. Device specifications are collected directly from the device manufacturer's website. Informed Shoppers also features video reviews from prominent creators. All other resources will be credited at the bottom of each device page.
